The Union

The Union — Built by lifters who were tired of waiting for a rack.

Barbell Union started in 2016 in a Ghitorni basement with four platforms, a chalk bucket and a whiteboard of totals. We were commercial-gym refugees — sick of queuing for the one squat rack, sick of "no chalk" signs, sick of trainers who had never pulled a heavy deadlift in their lives.

So we built the room we wanted to train in. Calibrated plates that don't lie. Platforms you can actually miss a lift on. Coaches who compete, log their own numbers on the same board as you, and coach the squat, bench and deadlift like the technical skills they are.

No mirrors-and-influencer theatre. No monthly "transformation" gimmicks. Just iron, a plan, and a total that goes up. Everyone from a first-time 40kg squatter to a 700kg-total competitor trains under the same roof — that's the union part.
