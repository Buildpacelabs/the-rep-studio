The studio

We built a nightclub
that happens to burn 600 calories.

Cadence started in 2021 when two ex-club promoters and a national-level cyclist got tired of grey, fluorescent gyms. So we blacked out the windows, wired in a proper sound system, and hung the room in magenta and cyan.

Every ride is choreographed to a single continuous set — no awkward gaps, no counting reps, just a coach on the podium calling the climbs while the whole room chases the drop together. You leave dripping, grinning and already texting the group chat about tomorrow's booking.

No mirrors on the front wall. No leaderboard shaming. Just you, the beat, and 39 people who showed up to move. That's the whole philosophy.

Reyna & Kabir Founders, Cadence Cycle
