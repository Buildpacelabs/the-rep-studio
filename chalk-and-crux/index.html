Our story

We built the wall we always wanted to fall off.

Chalk & Crux started with three friends, one dusty warehouse in Jayanagar, and a shared frustration: Bengaluru had the climbers but nowhere friendly to learn. So in 2021 we bolted our first panels, set our first ropes-free problems, and opened the doors.

Bouldering is climbing stripped to its most social self — no harness, no ropes, just short powerful problems, thick mats, and everyone shouting beta at everyone else. You fall a hundred times. Then you don't. That moment — the send — is what we chase together.

  • Routes reset weekly, so the wall never gets boring.
  • Coaches on the floor, not locked in an office.
  • A community that celebrates your first V0 as loudly as a V8.

Find your grade

Every colour is a story. Pick where yours starts.

Our problems are colour-coded by difficulty across six zones. Never climbed before? You start on Chalk and work up. Projecting V8? The purple wall is waiting.

  1. Chalk VB Never climbed? Start right here. Big jugs, gentle walls.
  2. Green V0–V1 Finding your feet. Learning to trust the rubber.
  3. Yellow V2–V3 Getting pumped. Real moves, real fun.
  4. Blue V4–V5 Proper problems. Body tension required.
  5. Coral V6–V7 Crux territory. This is where the shouting starts.
  6. Purple V8+ The project wall. Come back all week. Then send.
