Why the block

A box, not a gym floor

We opened Cinderblock in a stripped-back warehouse off Mundhwa–Kharadi Road because Pune had plenty of mirrors and not enough places to actually get capable. No machines lined up in rows. Just a rig, real barbells, a wall for wall-balls, and a coach who knows your name and your last score.

The whiteboard is the boss. One workout, one clock, one room full of people chasing the same thing at completely different levels — and a coach whose whole job is to scale the work so it fits you today. That's the promise: it hits hard, and it fits anyone.

  • 01Coached, always. Every class is led. You never guess, you never lift alone.
  • 02Scaled, honestly. Rx, Scaled or Foundations — same stimulus, your capacity.
  • 03Measured, weekly. Scores on the board so progress is a number, not a vibe.
