Our philosophy

Recovery isn't the soft option. It's where the work sets in.

We opened Coldfront because Bengaluru trains hard and rarely recovers on purpose. Hard sessions, long desk days and short sleep leave the nervous system stuck in high gear. Cold, heat and pressure — applied with intent — pull it back down.

Every protocol here is built on the same idea: give the body a clear, controlled signal and it adapts. Cold constricts, heat opens, compression flushes, breath steadies. We just sequence it, coach it, and keep the room quiet enough to feel it work.

  • 01 Measured, coached protocols — never a cold shock and a shrug.
  • 02 Clinical-grade kit, spa-calm room, zero gym noise.
  • 03 A plan that fits around training, not the other way round.

How a visit works

Contrast, done right, in four calm steps.

  1. 1

    Check-in & read

    A two-minute chat on how you slept, trained and feel today. Your coach sets the temperatures and times to match.

  2. 2

    Go cold

    Cryo chamber or plunge first. We coach the breath, hold the clock, and keep you calm through the shiver.

  3. 3

    Turn the heat on

    Into the infrared sauna to open everything back up. Repeat the loop for a true contrast effect if the session calls for it.

  4. 4

    Flush & leave lighter

    Finish on compression or mobility, rehydrate in the lounge, and walk out with a lower heart rate than you came in with.
