The Code

We built a room,
not a showroom.

Forge & Iron started in 2017 in a converted warehouse off 100 Feet Road, because the founders were tired of gyms that felt like nightclubs. No queues for the squat rack, no thumping "motivation," no equipment that snaps under real load.

What we kept is simple: calibrated iron, competition benches and platforms, and coaches who actually watch your set. What we threw out is everything else. You come in, you warm up, you put weight on the bar, and you leave a little stronger than yesterday.

Strength here is a practice, not a January resolution. The people who thrive at Forge are the ones who understand that the win is showing up on the day you don't feel like it.

Training

Pick your path under the bar

Every programme is coached and progressive — you always know your next lift and your next weight. Start where you are; we move you forward.

01 / Barbell

Strength Foundations

Learn the squat, bench, deadlift and press properly. Six coached weeks to build a base that holds under real weight.

Beginner · 6 weeks
02 / Barbell

Powerlifting Club

Peaking blocks, opener strategy and platform practice for lifters chasing a total. Meet-prep on request.

Advanced · Ongoing
03 / Conditioning

Iron Engine

Barbell complexes, sled pushes and carries. Strength that doesn't gas out at rep five. Small groups, big output.

All levels · 45 min
04 / Olympic

Snatch & Clean

Weightlifting technique for the snatch and clean & jerk. Positions first, load later, mobility throughout.

Intermediate · 60 min

Private Coaching

Fully bespoke programming and hands-on cues. Return from injury, break a plateau, or chase a specific number.

By appointment

Mobility & Reset

Hips, shoulders and thoracic work that keeps you lifting. Forty minutes that protect every session in your week.

All levels · 40 min
