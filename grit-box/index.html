The Box

Built raw,
run right.

We started Grit Box in 2018 in a bare Baner warehouse — a few barbells, a pull-up rig welded by a friend, and a chalkboard where we scrawled the day's work. No mirrors, no chrome, no membership you forget you're paying for.

The idea was simple: teach real people to move well and pull heavy, then hand them a workout that scales from a nervous first-timer to a Hyrox finisher — same class, same clock, same room.

Seven years on, the shelves are stacked and the crowd is loud, but the rule hasn't changed. You show up, the coach has your back, and the board tells you exactly what to do.
