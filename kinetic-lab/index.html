01 The method

We don't guess. We test, then train.

Kinetic Lab started in a HITEC City warehouse in 2019, when a physio and a track coach got tired of programmes written on vibes. We wired the floor with timing gates and force plates, and made one rule: every block of training has to move a number we can measure.

  1. A

    Assess

    A 90-minute lab session: movement screen, sprint gates, jump & strength testing. You leave with a baseline scorecard.

  2. B

    Programme

    Your coach designs a block around your gaps — speed, power, engine or resilience — with weekly loads mapped to your data.

  3. C

    Coach

    Every session is coached on the floor. Velocity targets on the bar, split times on the track, cues on your movement.

  4. D

    Re-test

    At six weeks we re-run the lab. The numbers show what worked; the next block adapts. Progress you can read, not feel.
