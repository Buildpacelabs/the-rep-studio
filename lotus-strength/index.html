Why Lotus

We started because the weights room was never built for us.

Lotus Strength began in a borrowed corner of a Kalyani Nagar studio in 2019, when our founder Meera got tired of hovering near the treadmills, waiting for a squat rack she felt allowed to use. So she built the room she wanted: proper barbells, coaches who explain the why, and no mirrors screaming at you.

Six years on we're a full studio in Koregaon Park with three programmes, four coaches and a community that shows up for each other. We teach strength as a skill — progressive, measurable and yours to keep for life.

  • 01 Coaching over correction — you learn the lift, not just do it.
  • 02 Small groups so nobody trains unseen.
  • 03 Progress you can measure, week on week.
