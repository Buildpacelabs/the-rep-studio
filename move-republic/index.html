Why we exist

A gym that never felt like one

Move Republic started because our founder quit three gyms in one year — treadmills were quiet, joyless places, and none of them stuck.

So she rented a mirrored room above a Bandra bakery, plugged in a speaker, and taught eight friends a Bollywood routine on a Sunday morning. Nobody checked a step counter. Everybody came back the next week.

Six years on, that room is a proper studio with a sprung floor, a wall of mirrors and a playlist that never stops. We believe fitness should feel like a Friday night — sweaty, loud, and impossible to do with a straight face. You don't need rhythm. You need forty-five minutes and a willingness to be a little ridiculous.

Rhea Kulkarni
Founder & head choreographer
