Since 2019

A gym built around three lifts, not fifty machines.

Platform 45 started as one deadlift platform, a calibrated bar and a whiteboard nailed to the wall. We named it for the plate you chase your whole lifting life — the red 45 that turns a working set into a record.

There is no mirror wall, no cable jungle, no queue for the leg press. Just power racks, mono-lifts, calibrated iron and people who know your opener. You get stronger here because everything in the room is pointed at one number: your total.

  • 0% of members hit a new PR inside their first twelve weeks
  • 0 competition platforms & power racks under one roof

The floor

Six platforms. One standard.

Every station is set up to competition spec, so the numbers you hit in training are the numbers you can trust on meet day.

  • 01

    Deadlift platforms

    Four dedicated pulling stations with rubber-topped decks and calibrated plates that let you drop a heavy single without wrecking the bar.

  • 02

    Competition mono-lift

    A commanded squat rack with the same walkout, depth and rack-height feel you will meet on the platform at a sanctioned event.

  • 03

    Calibrated bars & iron

    Power bars, a stiff bench bar and a deadlift bar — all sleeved, spun and matched to certified competition plates.

  • 04

    The board on the wall

    Every attempt over a house standard goes up on the PR board. If you pull it, you own it, and your name stays until someone takes it.
