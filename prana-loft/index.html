Our practice

Founded on the idea that slower is stronger.

Prana Loft began in 2016 as a rooftop practice for a handful of friends who were tired of yoga that felt like a workout with a deadline. We wanted a room that gave time back — where the teacher watches you, not the clock, and where a beginner and a decade-long practitioner can breathe in the same circle.

Today we're a small studio in Tapovan, five minutes from the Ganga. Wooden floors, tall windows, mountain light. No mirrors, no scoreboards, no rush. Just breath, movement, and a room built to slow you down.

Classes

Find the pace your body is asking for.

Every class is drop-in friendly and open to all levels. Not sure where to start? Come to a Hatha Foundations or Morning Vinyasa and we'll guide you.

Flow · 60 min

Morning Vinyasa Flow

A breath-led sequence to wake the spine and warm the whole body. Steady, not frantic — you'll leave clear-headed, not wiped out.

Stillness · 45 min

Candlelit Meditation

Guided breath and silent sitting by candlelight. A soft place to land in the evening and let the day settle before you head home.

Foundations · 75 min

Hatha Foundations

Classic postures held with care and clear alignment cues. The best possible start if you're new, coming back, or rebuilding strength.

Restore · 75 min

Yin & Restore

Long, supported holds with bolsters and blankets. Deep tissue release for tight hips and quiet nervous systems.

Breath · 45 min

Pranayama & Breathwork

Learn to breathe on purpose — techniques to steady the mind, lift energy or wind it down. Suitable for every level.

Sunrise · 60 min

Rooftop Sunrise Flow

A gentle standing flow on the terrace as the valley lights up. Limited mats — first light, first served.
