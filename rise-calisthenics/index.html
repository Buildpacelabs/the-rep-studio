Who we are

Strength you can
carry anywhere

We started on a rusty park bar with three friends and a whiteboard of goals.

Rise Calisthenics grew out of Bengaluru's outdoor street-workout scene — the kind where nobody cares what you lift, only what you can move your own body through. We built the gym we always wanted: German-steel rigs, chalk in the air, and coaches who actually program your progressions.

No mirrors to pose in. No leg-day queue. Just bars, rings, parallettes and a plan that takes you from hanging on for dear life to holding a clean lever. Beginners and street-workout veterans train the same floor — and that mix is the whole point.

  • Progressions, not guessworkEvery skill mapped rep-by-rep so you always know your next step.
  • Built for real bodiesScaled entry points for absolute beginners through advanced athletes.
  • Community over egoTrain alongside people chasing the same holds you are.
  • Coach-led floorA qualified coach on the mats every coached session, every day.
