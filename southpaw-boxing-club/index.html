The corner we built

A boxing gym, not a boxing-themed gym.

Southpaw started in a leaking basement off Pusa Road with two heavy bags, a borrowed ring and a coach who refused to teach anyone a hook until their jab was honest. Twelve years on, the leak is fixed and the ring is ours — but the rule hasn't changed.

We don't do choreographed "boxing-inspired" cardio. You'll learn stance, footwork and the four punches properly, hit real bags until your shoulders learn the shots, and — when you're ready and only when you're ready — you'll spar under a coach who knows when to stop the round.

  • Everyone starts on the fundamentals. White-collar beginner or ex-competitor, day one is footwork.
  • Small groups, real eyes on you. Never more than eight to a coach on the mitts.
  • Sparring is earned, supervised and optional. Nobody gets thrown to the wolves.

What you'll train

Classes & programmes

Seven ways into the sport, from your very first wrap to your first bout. Every session is coached start to finish.

Start here

Fundamentals

Stance, guard, footwork and the four straight punches, drilled slow and correct. No experience assumed. This is where every member begins.

60 min · All levels
One-on-one eyes

Pad Work

Rounds on the focus mitts with a coach reading and correcting every combination. The fastest way to sharpen timing, accuracy and defence.

60 min · Small group
Earned & supervised

Sparring

Controlled, coach-refereed rounds once you've got the basics and the gear. Intensity matched to your level — you spar people, not egos.

75 min · By clearance

Bag & Conditioning

Heavy-bag rounds folded into a boxer's strength circuit — core, grip, footwork and gas-tank work. The engine behind everything else.

50 min · All levels

Women's Boxing

A women-only room coached by Neha Rawat. Same technical standard, built to be the most welcoming session on the timetable.

60 min · Women only

White-Collar Boxing

An eight-week course for working professionals who want to actually learn the sport after work — from first jab to a friendly in-house round.

8-week course
Invite & assessment

Competition Squad

For members chasing amateur bouts. Extra rounds, film review, weight management and a corner team on fight night. Ask a coach to be assessed.
