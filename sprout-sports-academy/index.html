Our story

We started with one muddy field and a simple belief.

Sprout began when Coach Meera watched her own daughter dread PE class — bored, benched, and convinced she "wasn't sporty". So we built the opposite of that: a place where every child gets the ball, gets a turn, and gets cheered on by name.

Ten years on, our little academy in Baner runs on the same idea. Movement first, medals later. We teach the fundamentals of a dozen sports through play, so kids build confidence, coordination and friendships that outlast any season.

  • 🌱 Play before pressure. Skills grow when kids are having fun.
  • 🤝 Everyone plays. No benching, no favourites, no left-out kids.
  • 🛡️ Safety you can see. Certified coaches, safe ratios, secure pick-up.

Programmes by age

Find the right start for your child

45-minute sessions · 2× a week

Little Sprouts

Wobbles, giggles and first goals. Our youngest movers build balance, coordination and body-confidence through parachute games, mini obstacle courses and soft-ball sports — always at their own pace.

  • Fundamental movement: run, jump, throw, catch, balance
  • Turn-taking, listening and gentle teamwork
  • Parent-and-me option for brand-new starters
🐣

Focus: confidence & coordination

60-minute sessions · 2–3× a week

Junior Movers

The multi-sport sampler. Kids rotate through football, basketball, athletics and gymnastics across the term, discovering what lights them up while learning real technique in a friendly, no-pressure setting.

  • Sport-specific fundamentals across 4+ disciplines
  • Small-sided games and mini-tournaments
  • Goal-setting they can actually feel proud of
🏃

Focus: try everything, find your spark

75-minute sessions · 3× a week

Rising Stars

Where a favourite sport becomes a specialism. Rising Stars pick a lead sport, train the details, and play in our friendly inter-academy fixtures — with strength and mobility work built in to keep growing bodies happy.

  • Choose a lead sport + one supporting sport
  • Age-appropriate strength, agility & recovery
  • Match play, positions and reading the game
⭐

Focus: skill, teamwork & grit

90-minute sessions · 3–4× a week

Academy Elite

For the committed. Elite athletes follow a periodised plan in their chosen sport, with video feedback, fitness testing and pathways toward school and district-level selection — all while keeping the joy in the game.

  • Sport specialisation with individual development plans
  • Strength & conditioning fundamentals, safely taught
  • Guidance toward trials, tournaments & scholarships
🏆

Focus: performance & pathways

🛡️ Safe hands, every session

Parents don't just leave a child with us — they trust us. So we earn it, every single day.

  • Background-checked coaches. Every coach is police-verified and child-safeguarding trained.
  • First-aid on site. A qualified first-aider is present at all times, with kits and a paediatric protocol.
  • Safe ratios. Never more than six young movers to a coach in our little groups.
  • Secure pick-up. Authorised-guardian sign-out and a fenced, monitored play area.
