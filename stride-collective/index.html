01 — The Club

We started with six pairs of shoes and one loop of Cubbon Park.

In 2016, a handful of us were tired of running alone — dodging traffic, skipping the alarm, quietly quitting every January. So we agreed to meet at the park gate at dawn, no matter what. Six became sixteen. Sixteen became a movement.

Stride Collective is built on one stubborn idea: nobody gets dropped. We run in pace groups, we wait at the top of the hill, and we finish together. Whether your target is a sub-3 marathon or simply getting round the lake without walking, there's a group here running your speed — and a coach who knows your name.

  • → Pace groups from 7:30/km to 4:15/km, every run
  • → Certified coaches on the road, not on an app
  • → Coffee, stretching and stories after — that's the point
