Our ground

Built on sand, not on subscriptions

Terrain started with eight friends, one kettlebell rack and a stretch of Ozran beach nobody was using at 5:45am. We were tired of stale air-conditioned gyms and rooms full of strangers ignoring each other. So we took it outside.

Seven years on, the format hasn't changed: we meet before the crowds, we train hard against the elements, and we finish in the sea. Every session is coach-led and scaled to you — whether it's your first push-up or your fiftieth trail run.

— Kabir RaneFounder & head coach

Three grounds, one tribe

Where we train

We move with the tide and the light. Your session confirmation tells you exactly where to meet — all within ten minutes of central Anjuna.

◈ Ozran Beach

The Sand Box

Our home ground. Firm morning sand, flat sprint lanes and the sea for the finish. Strength and HIIT live here.

◈ Chapora Trails

The Dirt Line

Red-earth paths winding up to the fort. Where Coastal Engine and the Saturday long run get their hills.

◈ Vagator Cliff

The High Ground

Clifftop grass with a view of the whole bay. Sunset Burn and mobility work close out the week up here.
