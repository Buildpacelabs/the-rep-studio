01 — The studio

A room built for control, not spectacle.

The Reformer Room began in 2019 above a quiet lane off Waterfield Road, when our founder went looking for a place to rehabilitate a stubborn lower back and found only crowded, hurried classes. So she built the opposite: pale wood, soft light, six reformers and never a seventh.

We teach classical control layered with contemporary progressions — the kind of precise, low-impact work that carries into how you sit, lift, run and breathe. No mirrors to perform for. No leaderboard. Just attentive coaching that meets your body where it is today.

  • Small groups, so your form is actually watched.
  • Beginner-safe on every reformer class.
  • Rehab-informed instructors for niggles and pre/post-natal.
