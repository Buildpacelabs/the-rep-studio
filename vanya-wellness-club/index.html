The idea of Vanya

We built a forest in the city — a place to train hard, then go quiet.

Mumbai never stops. Vanya was made as the counterweight — a club where effort and ease sit side by side. Lift in a room built for it, swim lengths in still, warm water, then dissolve into a spa that asks nothing of you.

Membership is deliberately limited. Fewer people, more room, no queues for the rack you want. Everything from the linen to the lighting is chosen so that an hour here feels longer than it should.

  • 01 Capped membership, uncrowded floors
  • 02 Coaches who remember your name and your knees
  • 03 Spa & recovery as standard, not an upsell

The club, room by room

Five sanctuaries under one roof.

Move between them freely. Membership opens every door — the whole club, every day.

The Strength Floor

Full free-weight racks, platforms, plate-loaded machines and a dedicated conditioning zone.

Mineral Pool

A warm, low-chlorine lap pool under glass, with cold plunge and a heated relaxation deck.

The Spa & Sauna

Nordic sauna, steam, and treatment suites for massage, recovery and Ayurvedic ritual.

Movement Studio

Sprung-floor studio for yoga, reformer and small-group work, wrapped in natural light.

Members' Lounge

Quiet workspace, a wellness café and a terrace over the sea — for the hours between sessions.
